Our Ideal Jobs

 

Aidyn: Reese Tech Data Analyst
Product Data Analyst at reecetech requires employees to produce information for improving products and delivering user-based services for customers through the Reece maX app, employees will need to produce large data sets and analyse the work of peers to review one another to produce further insights from data and inform development of a product within the maX application better the experience at the company, documenting the data assets and collaborating with customers and staff to expand and build data analysis methods. This position is appealing to me as I find data management interesting and enjoy finding the information that can be extracted from data, giving me insights into the data and how it may predict/show events that will allow me to develop more changes for the maX application.

Mun Kit: Robert Half Support Technician
This job is all about maintaining, monitoring, and troubleshooting devices and systems including hardware and software for the specified company so that systems are ready to use for other users to do work on. This job is appealing to me as I enjoy problem solving and prefer working in a team to solve problems. The skills, qualifications and experience required for the job are as follows:
- Ability to analyse and document business requirements/improvement opportunities.
- End to End Project management skills
- IP Networking (firewall & router configuration/patching)
- Microsoft 365/Office experience & administration
- Awareness of ICT Cyber security risk and compliance assessments
- Hospitality experience highly desirable
- Driver’s license and car

Owen: Cisco Network Engineer
A Cisco Network Engineer has the job of developing and maintaining networks which run on Cisco hardware products. They cover a variety of aspects in relation to the network, such as creating and configuring networks, have the ability to troubleshoot any issues with it, and also ensure maximum security of the network. The position shown above specifically focuses on the infrastructure of the network in terms of day-to-day use, whilst also ensuring that the network and security operations are running as efficiently and effectively as possible. The focus on the infrastructure side of network engineering is what appeals to me especially, as I am curious of the latest technology that is being developed to allow for businesses of any size to run the best they can. In terms of skills for the job, they include:
- Having the ability to manage LAN/WAN networks
- Managing voice gateways, fibre channel switches and network security
- At least 3 years of recent experience as a network engineer
- Solid communication, analytical and problem-solving skills for engaging with business users and project stakeholders.
- Regarding qualifications, all that is listed is having current Cisco Certifications in Routing and Switching and/or Data Centre.

Zachary: 8T8 Technologies IT Specialist Technician/ System Engineer
This job listing is for an IT Specialist Technician/System Engineer at 8T8 Technologies located in Melbourne. The position primarily involves working with networks and systems but also includes administration and general technical support. The full list of areas covered in the job includes working with file servers, proxy servers, LAN/WAN/Intranets, desktop/laptop hardware, software, outlook Email supports, remote management services, departmental applications, and technology rollouts for one or multiple schools. This job appeals to me specifically because of the branches of IT that it covers, including networking and technical support. Mainly, the ability to help members of a company and clients with their IT problems is what drives me to study IT as I have a passion for helping others. In terms of more specific skills, the position requires experience and in-depth knowledge of Microsoft Windows Server products, network infrastructure, Active Directory Domains, popular operating systems, and Google apps. In terms of general work skills, good communication, documentation skills, excellent time management skills and the ability to prioritise tasks are preferred.

Harrison: Petrosys Database administrator/ manager
This position is for a database administrator and data manager at Petrosys Pty. Ltd., an Adelaide-based company that specialises in providing geoscience products for the gas and oil industry. This role would include working with clients in order to provide database/data management solutions and providing regular administrative work and advice to clients regarding the data they are collecting. Furthermore, it would also involve the use of an internal database at Petrosys in order to manage databases and aid the research and development process.

Teodore: Creative Director - Dice
As a Creative Director it is your job to bridge together multiple departments to create a unified creative vision that fills the intended project goals. You essentially act as the authority over aspects of the game such as the gameplay, visual style, story, audio assets, cinematics, and marketing materials. DICE is a subsidiary of the controversial Electronic Studios, but they have developed many first-person shooters that I love to play such as the rebooted STAR WARS BATTLEFRONT series. That part that is most appealing to me about being a Creative Director is the entire process of creating and developing a project. I love exploring all my creative possibilities, seeing what sticks and what does not and trying to find more ways to be fresh and innovative. I want to slowly evolve these ideas and create a strong vision of what the completed product should look like. But the most important part is putting in the work to make this vision a reality, when I am dedicated and passionate about something, I will put my blood, sweat and tears into any project to try and realise my ideas and see it through right till the end.
- Experience in shipping at least 1 AAA title as a creative director
- +8 years’ experience in game development These qualifications are important as DICE is a well-respected AAA publisher, they need to make sure that those who manage their projects are of the highest standards and already comfortable to do what is asked of them.
But in addition to these qualifications, I need to be quite comfortable with public speaking to represent the creative studios and also good with structure and documentation to maintain a high level of quality and process control.

Aidyn and Harrison’s ideal jobs focus on a different aspect of the I.T industry, databases and datasets. With their ideal jobs being Data Analyst and Data Administrator, it is obvious that similarities will be present in these jobs. Both deal with interpreting datasets, and also how to deconstruct them into something meaningful. However, Aidyn’s Data Analyst ideal job interprets datasets with the use of improving and developing products for the company of Reecetech. On the other hand, Harrison’s Data Admin job interprets datasets with the purpose of aiding in research to advise clients on the data that they are collecting.

The wildcard ideal job here is Teodore’s, which is a Creative Director of a AAA video game publisher DICE. To dissect this role, similarities in jobs can be seen in the requirement to have good teamwork and team leadership skills which is of course imperative as a successful creative director. Mun Kit and Owen’s ideal jobs also require good team work skills to fulfill their roles. Zach and Teodore’s roles also require good ability in documentation and time management. In contrast, Teodore’s ideal job requires not just 8+ years experience in the video game industry, but also fantastic public speaking abilities to represent the video game studio.

Overall, it is apparent that these ideal job roles all share some similarities, but also many many differences. This is a testament to how vast the IT industry is, and how all of us want to follow different career avenues.
